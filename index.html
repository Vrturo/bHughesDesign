Bobbie

My name is Bobbie Hughes, I am an aspiring Designer who comes from a small town in Iowa. I moved to San Francisco in 2014 to pursue my creative passion of design and architecture. Iâ€™m currently obtaining my BFA in Interior Architecture & Design at the Academy of Art University and due to graduate August 2017.

At the Academy I have been fortunate enough to learn a variety of skillsets under extreme time crunches with phenomenal instructors. Throughout the years I have found a love for Hospitality Design and Industrial Style Design.
